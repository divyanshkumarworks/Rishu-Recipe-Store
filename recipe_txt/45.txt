Title: Corn Salad Recipe

prep time: 34

Description: Juicy sweet corn kernels are used in many recipes and commonly found in salads. Corn salad is one such simple recipe where sweet corn kernels are mixed with various fresh vegetables and herbs and sprinkled with pepper and salt. It is also a great accompaniment for lunch and dinner.

Ingredient: Ingredients: Salt to taste, 1 Green Chilli, finely chopped, 1/4 teaspoon Black Pepper Powder, 1 cup fresh or frozen Corn Kernels, boiled (approx. 1 large Corn Cob), 1/3 cup diced Cucumber, 1/3 cup diced Tomato, (1 medium tomato), 2 tablespoons diced Onion, 2 tablespoons finely chopped Cilantro (Coriander Leaves), 1 tablespoon Olive Oil, optional, 1 tablespoon Lemon Juice (or Lime Juice)

Directions:
step-1: Boil the corn in salted water in a pressure cooker for 4-whistles over medium flame or grill the corn. Let them cool to room temperature.
step-2: Remove corn kernels from the corn using a sharp knife and transfer them to a large bowl.
step-3: Add diced cucumber, diced tomato, diced onion, chopped green chilli and cilantro(coriander leaves).
step-4: Pour 1 tablespoon lime juice over it.
step-5: Pour 1 tablespoon olive oil over it.
step-6: Sprinkle 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder and salt to taste.
step-7: Toss gently to mix well. Corn salad is ready. Serve it as an accompaniment.
